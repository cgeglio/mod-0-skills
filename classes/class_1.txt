Class Glass

Attributes:
color (string)
material (string)
maximumVolume (integer)
currentVolume (integer)
stem (boolean)

Methods:
calculatePercentFull (currentVolume / maximumVolume)
fill (increases currentVolume by 1)
drinkFrom (decreases currentVolume by 1)
spill (decreases currentVolume by 2)
changeColor (changes color attribute)
